Position

Stella d'Argento Bed and Breakfast Porto Santo Stefano is 3 km from the Spanish Fortress . Also, this property is located approximately 10 minutes by car from Cala Grande Beach.

Rooms

The accommodation includes rooms with a bidet, a separate toilet and a shower, as well as a patio, a mini fridge and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Guests can make use of a mini fridge, and relax with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.
Some rooms have a sea view and others a garden view, but all benefit from a splendid terrace overlooking the sea, ideal for breakfast and an aperitif.

Services

The rooms are welcoming and offer air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, TV, fridge, toiletries, hairdryer, free covered parking space and breakfast included.
The B&B is immersed in a garden of olive trees and oleanders and is 5 km from the town centre, where there are many restaurants specializing in Tuscan cuisine and fresh fish.
