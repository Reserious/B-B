Benvenuto

Stella d'argento is a unique location, with a breathtaking sea view of the island of Giglio and the island of Montecristo, about 5 km from the town center. The rooms have a sea view and others a garden view, but all they benefit from a splendid terrace overlooking the sea, ideal for breakfast and an aperitif. The rooms are welcoming and offer air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, TV, fridge, breakfast included, covered parking space, bathroom products, and hairdryer. Stella d'argento is located near the most beautiful coves of the Argentario such as Cala del chalk, Cala grande, Cala del bove.
